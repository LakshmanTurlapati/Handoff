<objective>
Build the durable device-session and revoke foundation for Phase 4.

Purpose: Phase 4 cannot deliver safe device management until the product stops treating `cm_device_session` as a cookie-only trust object. This plan introduces the real `device_sessions` persistence, hardens principal validation, and adds the explicit device-management and revoke flows that later audit/reconnect work depends on.

Output: Shared Postgres/Drizzle repositories, claim-time `device_sessions` persistence, durable remote-principal checks, a mobile device-management screen, and a revoke flow that immediately ends matching live browser access.
</objective>
